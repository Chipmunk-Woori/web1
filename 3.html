Alice's Adventures in Wonderland

Scene 3

Alice : Now I can go to the garden! but what's this? (tastes water) Salt water? Maybe it's the sea.
이제 내가 정원으로 갈 수 있겠구나! 그런데 이게 뭐지? 소금물? 어쩌면 이것이 바닷물일지도 모르겠다.

(She starts to swim. Sound of something splashing in the pool.)

Alice : Someone is swimming! Oh, it's a hippo! No, it's a mouse! But since now I am small, it seems huge! I will talk to the mouse, maybe it can answer me!
누군가가 수영하고 있어! 오, 하마구나! 아니야, 쥐다! 그런데 지금은 내가 작다보니, 엄청나게 커 보여! 쥐에게 말을 걸어봐야겠어. 나에게 대답해 줄 수도 있잖아!

Alice : Tell me, mouse, is this the sea?
쥐야, 알려줘. 이것이 바다니?

(The mouse looked at her, but it doesn't answer.
